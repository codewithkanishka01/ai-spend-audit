We cross-reference your current tools, team size, and use cases against the latest public pricing pages and hidden enterprise tiers. We identify redundancies (like paying for both ChatGPT Team and Claude Team) and surface cheaper alternatives or bulk credit opportunities.

**2. Is this actually free?**
Yes. The audit is 100% free. We make money if you choose to procure discounted AI credits through Credex after your audit. There's no obligation to buy.

**3. Do I need to connect my bank account or AWS?**
No. You just manually input your current tool stack (e.g., Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Anthropic API) and your estimated monthly spend. We do the math instantly.

**4. What if we are already optimized?**
If your stack is lean and you're on the right plans, we'll tell you honestly. You can still subscribe to get notified when new AI pricing models or credit programs apply to your stack.

**5. How accurate is the pricing data?**
Our pricing database is updated weekly. We monitor the official pricing pages of all major AI vendors, ensuring our recommendations are always based on current market rates.
